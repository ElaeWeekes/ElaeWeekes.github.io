Biography

Eleazer (Elae) Weekes is a singer-songwriter from Boston Massachusetts. Being raised in a very musical family, Elae began singing at the age of six and began writing music in middle school. He began singing professionally with various gospel groups throughout his high school years before songwriting and performing original music his freshman year of college.

Elae has worked with over several artists and producers around the world. He has worked with producers Cisco the Producer, Abraham LisSon, and Artist K.I Breaux just to name a few.

In the summer of 2017, Elae released his first extended play (EP) project entitled “In My Feelings” including production from both Gregory Weekes and Francios Noel. In this project he featured Elizabeth Auguste and Clyde Black in his compilation of songs which displayed deep emotion and vivid poetry. Elae is currently working on new music planned to release in 2018 along with several performances throughout the year.
